Tools

Discipline - Each journal is classified as belonging to one, and only one, discipline. Disciplines were derived by our team from the original three-level classification system from 1Science/Science-Metrix using Domain-->Field-->Subfield. The original Science-Metrix classification had 6 domains, 22 fields, and 176 subfields; we have attempted to map the Fields more closely to areas of study at UVA. These resulting “Disciplines” were formed from the Fields by adding or removing Subfields. There are 20 mutually exclusive Disciplines.

Interdisciplinary journals can be challenging to locate in this Discipline hierarchy; therefore, we have provided a “Which discipline is my journal in?” link under the Disciplines dropdown menu within the Journals by Discipline Tool.

Provider - Each journal is made available to UVA by one or more providers (publisher, aggregator, or archive). The list of providers include publishers like Elsevier, Sage, and SpringerNature, but also aggregators like EBSCO and ProQuest, as well as the archive JSTOR. Some individual journals change publishers over time, or are available both through a publisher and an aggregator/archive. This means that a single journal may show up in the listing for more than one provider. There are 35 providers in the 1Science dataset.
